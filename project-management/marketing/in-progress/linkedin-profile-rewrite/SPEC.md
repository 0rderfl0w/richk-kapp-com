# LinkedIn Profile Rewrite — Richard Kappel

## Context

Richard's LinkedIn profile currently only promotes Build to Own Club. Two higher-ticket revenue streams are invisible:
- **Perspective Advisory** (richkapp.com/advisory) — strategic advisory for founders and businesses
- **Perspective AI** (richkapp.com/ai) — done-for-you AI tool building for SMBs ($5K-$25K)

The profile needs to speak to three audiences in priority order:
1. Business owners who want to hire Richard (advisory + AI) — highest ticket
2. Founders who need strategic guidance — advisory
3. Builders who want to learn — Build to Own Club

---

## Headline

**Current:**
Founder, Build to Own Club | Shipped a SaaS with AI, no dev team

**Proposed:**
Founder, Perspective | AI Advisory & Tools for Businesses | Build to Own Club

Why: Leads with the business. "AI Advisory & Tools for Businesses" speaks directly to the paying client. B2O is still there but it's not the lead.

**Alternative (punchier):**
I help businesses work smarter with AI. I teach everyone else how to build their own.

Why: Two audiences, one sentence each. The business owner sees "help businesses." The builder sees "teach everyone else." But this loses the company name.

**Recommended:** Option 2. The punchy version. Company names go in the Experience section. The headline is a billboard, not a business card.

---

## About

**Current:** Long narrative about gratitude, life chapters, permaculture, perspective shifts. Warm but no clear offer.

**Final Version:**

I started my first company in my twenties. Built it up. Sold it in Chicago. Then I did what most people only talk about.

I opened a hostel on the Camino de Santiago for pilgrims walking across Spain. Ran a permaculture farm. Founded a nonprofit that grew into a social enterprise across 11 countries with a 10-person dev team. Spent years mentoring founders building things that matter.

Then AI happened.

I built a full SaaS product. Alone. No dev team. No contractors. Something that used to cost six figures and a year of development, I did in a couple months.

That broke my brain a little. If I could do this, anyone could. And most businesses had no idea what was already possible.

Now I do two things.

**I build AI tools for businesses.** Automations, internal systems, workflows, customer-facing tools. The stuff that gets you 10+ hours back every week. You own everything I build.
→ richkapp.com/ai

**I teach people to build their own.** Build to Own Club is a private community where you learn AI by building real things. No coding needed. You build it, you own it, forever.
→ buildtoown.club

If you're building something worth talking about, let's connect.
